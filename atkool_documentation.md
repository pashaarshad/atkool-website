# ATKool Platform - Client Documentation

## Overview
ATKool is a comprehensive, multi-tenant School Management System designed to empower educational institutions with state-of-the-art tools for managing their daily operations. The platform offers an intuitive, premium interface (the "Royal Indigo" design system) to ensure an exceptional user experience for Super Admins, School Administrators, Teachers, and Students.

## Core Modules

### 1. Super Admin Dashboard
The command center for the platform owner.
- **Platform Analytics**: Real-time insights into the number of onboarded schools, active users, and platform revenue.
- **School Management**: Ability to create new school instances, generate unique credentials, and manage subscription plans.

### 2. School Admin Portal
Once a school is created by the Super Admin, the School Admin can log in to their dedicated portal to manage their institution.

#### Dashboard
- **Quick Stats**: Overview of total students, teachers, and pending approvals.
- **Quick Actions**: Shortcuts to add teachers, admit students, and view pending requests.

#### Teachers Management
- **Directory**: View all registered teachers.
- **Registration**: Add new teachers with their specific details and assign them to classes.

#### Students & Pending Students
- **Student Directory**: Comprehensive list of all enrolled students.
- **Pending Approvals**: A queue for new student registrations waiting for admin approval. Admins can approve or reject applications here.

#### Exams & Events
- **Exam Management**: Schedule new exams, set duration, marks, and view upcoming vs. completed exams.
- **Event Management**: Create school events (for all, teachers, or students) and upload event banners.

#### Communication
- **Send Message**: Bulk SMS/Email functionality to send announcements to specific groups (e.g., all parents, specific classes, or teachers).
- **Support**: A dedicated ticketing system for schools to raise issues with the platform's super admin or support team.

#### Billing & Plans
- **Overview & Plans**: Manage the school's current subscription plan, view available premium features, and handle billing details securely.

## Workflow: From Creation to Operation
1. **School Creation**: The Super Admin logs in, navigates to "Schools", and clicks "Add New School". They provide the school name, email, and password.
2. **School Login**: The newly created School Admin uses the provided credentials on the login page to access their dedicated dashboard.
3. **Onboarding Staff**: The School Admin adds Teachers via the Teachers module.
4. **Enrolling Students**: Students can be added manually or approved via the Pending Students list.
5. **Daily Operations**: The admin uses the portal to schedule exams, announce events, and communicate with parents and staff.
